Community Statement

The community of Tezos supporters is critical to the success of the Tezos protocol as well as the Tezos Foundation. As a unified voice of developers, community organizers, website resource creators, delegator services, and others involved we want to make sure that our voice is heard. The voice of the community.

We request the removal of Johann Gevers from the foundation. Independent of the results of the audit, the foundation needs another leader. There is too much distrust of Gevers within the community and negative publicity outside of the community. This is due to both internal factors in how Gevers has handled the foundation and external factors with other failed ventures like Monetas. The trust of Gevers within the community cannot be salvaged at this point.

The concerns about Gevers from the community include: lack of communication, delays in providing resources to development work, delays in organizing a PR team, delays in marketing efforts, delays in overall building of the foundation, inability to resolve disputes between the foundation and DLS, lack of experience running a large almost $1B institution.
